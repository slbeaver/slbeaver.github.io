Structure of the competition

The questions on the program will comply with the ‘Bebras standards’ - the International Challenge on Informatics and Computational Thinking, with the following major features.

  • Representing informatics concepts
  • Easily understandable
  • Solved within 3 minutes
  • Short, e.g. presentable at a single screen page
  • Solvable at computer without use of other software or paper and pencil
  • Independent from specific systems
  • Interesting and/or funny

The online contest will have 15 to 30 questions similar to the ones provided in Sample Questions page.

Each question will have a fixed time limit to be answered. Participants are expected to be present online on the contest platform 10 minutes prior to the contest start.

Students are expected to have a good internet connection throughout the contest.
