How to take Part

Basic Requirements

  1. You have to be a Sri Lankan student enrolled at a school
  2. You must be less than 19 years of age by 1st of January 2017

How to take part?

Keep in touch with this website. Once the contest registration opens, you will have to come and register yourself with some sort of proof material to prove your age and that you are a student.

Meanwhile, you can do the sample questions posted at ‘sample questions’ page, and try to solve the challeng questions posted on Beaver Facebook page.

The contest will be held online, you are recommended to find a place with a good internet connection, and the use of a laptop or a desktop computer is advised over the use of a mobile phone.

There will be a practice trial round, a week prior to the contest date, for you to familiarize with the contest system.

Important

No form of assistance is allowed during the contest and you are expected to solve all the problems by yourself. If any form of cheating is found, you will be banned from all future National Computational Thinking Contest contests.
